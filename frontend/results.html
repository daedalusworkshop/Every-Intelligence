How to Keep Your Writing Weird in the Age of AI

Katie Parrott / Working Overtime

→ Read the Essay

You're navigating the tension between creative freedom and the pressure to produce.

That's a challenge Katie Parrott explores directly. She found that the very tools meant to increase output can sometimes sterilize the creative process. In her essay, she wrestled with this, asking herself: "How do you keep your work unique and imbued with personality when AI can generate a thousand similar things in a second?" It's a crucial question for anyone working on the frontier.
